Business/ Product

Kids Against Hunger Canada

Kids Against Hunger Canada is a non-profit organization committed to reducing food insecurity not only in Canada but across the world.

This organization is on a mission to engage Canadians, especially the youth, in the fight against hunger and malnutrition.

Research

Problem Statement

Child hunger is a pervasive issue too extensive for a single entity or country to address. Kids Against Hunger Canada is on a mission to involve Canadians, particularly the youth, in the battle against hunger and malnutrition.

However, a significant challenge emerged - the organization's website lacked the necessary trustworthiness for potential donors. Outdated design and an overwhelming amount of information made donors skeptical. The key question: How can we boost contributions by enhancing transparency in the donation process and showcasing the charity's reputation and impact?

Iteration

To address the challenges, we embarked on a journey involving wireframing, refining and testing, analyzing key findings, A/B testing, and creating a seamless user experience. The process included:

  • Revamping the logo for better visibility
  • Enhancing navigation for improved user experience
  • Implementing a compelling landing page with a live meal counter
  • Highlighting financial transparency and spending through infographics
  • Streamlining the donation process into a concise 2-step form
